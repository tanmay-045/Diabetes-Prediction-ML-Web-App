Diabetes Prediction Form

This application is trained using the Pima Indians Diabetes Database, which contains health-related data from women aged 21 and older. The dataset includes important health parameters such as glucose levels, insulin, BMI, and others to predict diabetes.

The prediction model used here is a Logistic Regression model. This type of model is well-suited for binary classification problems such as predicting whether a person has diabetes or not. Logistic Regression works by estimating the probability that a given instance belongs to a certain class, in this case, diabetic or non-diabetic.









The model used has an approximate accuracy of 77% in predicting diabetes and relies on key health indicators to generate its predictions.
