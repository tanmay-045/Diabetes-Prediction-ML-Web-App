Diabetes Prediction Web App

This web application helps predict whether a person is diabetic based on specific health-related factors. The model, trained on real-world data, aims to assist in the early diagnosis of diabetes. Detecting diabetes early allows for better management and prevention of further complications.

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a condition in which the body cannot effectively process blood sugar (glucose), leading to high blood glucose levels. Insulin, a hormone produced by the pancreas, helps regulate glucose in the body, but in people with diabetes, this process is disrupted.

  • Did you know? More than 1 in 10 people globally are affected by diabetes.
  • Regular health check-ups can help catch diabetes in its early stages.
  • With proper management, many people with diabetes live healthy and fulfilling lives.

Early diagnosis and lifestyle changes can significantly reduce the risk of complications caused by diabetes. This tool helps evaluate your risk quickly and easily.
